# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, harassment-free, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in this project, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:
- communicate respectfully and professionally,
- provide constructive, evidence-based feedback,
- assume good faith and seek clarification before escalation,
- acknowledge uncertainty and limitations in technical claims,
- prioritize reproducibility and transparency.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct,
- personal attacks, insults, or intimidation,
- deliberate misrepresentation of results,
- sustained disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions,
- publishing private/confidential information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers via the repository contact channels. Please include relevant context and links/screenshots where possible.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant ethos.
